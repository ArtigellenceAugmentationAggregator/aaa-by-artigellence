AAA — MAKE THE BOT + LANDING PAGES LIVE ON YOUR REAL SITE
=========================================================

You have TWO things to add to your existing site. Both are copy-paste. No rebuild.

---------------------------------------------------------
PART 1 — THE CHAT BUBBLE (bot on every page)
---------------------------------------------------------
1. Upload  aaa-widget.js  to your repo root (same place as index.html).
2. On EVERY page you want the bot (start with index.html), add this ONE line
   just before the closing </body> tag:

   <script src="aaa-widget.js" defer></script>

That's it. A gold "Ask AAA" bubble appears bottom-right; click = the concierge opens,
greets the visitor, has Listen/voice, and answers about AAA. Same brain as agent.html.
Add the same line to your other pages (faq.html, services.html, etc.) to have it everywhere.

(agent.html can stay as a full-page version to link from ads / the landing pages. The
 bubble is the "greets everyone who lands" version you wanted.)

---------------------------------------------------------
PART 2 — THE INDUSTRIES SECTION (makes the 6 landing pages visible)
---------------------------------------------------------
Paste this block into index.html where you want it (a good spot: below your hero,
or above your footer). It links to all six industry pages. Styling is self-contained.

<!-- paste the <section class="aaa-industries"> ... </section> block from demo.html here -->
(Open demo.html, copy everything between the two comment lines
   "PASTE-IN BLOCK #2 : INDUSTRIES"  and the </section> that closes it,
 plus the matching CSS in the <style> under "INDUSTRIES SECTION".)

Simplest: just copy the whole styled block below into your page.
It works standalone.

---------------------------------------------------------
PART 3 — SEO: register the pages in sitemap.xml (2 min)
---------------------------------------------------------
Open your existing sitemap.xml and add these <url> entries so Google & AI index them:

  <url><loc>https://aaa.artigellence.com/agent.html</loc></url>
