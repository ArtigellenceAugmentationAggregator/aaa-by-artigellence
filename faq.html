What does AI automation cost for a small business, all-in?

Two paths, depending on whether you want ongoing support. Full engagement — Phase 0, Build and Retainer — is approximately A$70,500 in Year 1, with no upfront lump sums. Phase 0 is A$4,500 and fully refundable; the build phases are paid only on milestone delivery.

Phase 0 plus Build only is approximately A$28,500, and you keep all the agents and walk away. The honest caveat: agents drift over time without tuning, so if you don't have an internal person to own them, the retainer path works better.

How is my business data protected?

Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles compliant by design, aligned with the National AI Centre Guidance for AI Adoption.

A mutual NDA is signed before Phase 0 begins. Access to your existing systems is read-only. You own all tooling, and everything is documented.

What happens if I cancel — do I lose the work?

You own everything built. The agents are deployed in your own accounts — not the operator's. If the engagement ends, you keep the agents, the prompts, the documentation and the credentials, plus a full transition pack.

This isn't a vendor lock-in business. It's a working partnership.

How is this different from buying ChatGPT or Copilot myself?

Different problem. AI subscriptions often go unused because owners buy the tool but never build the workflow around it. AAA builds the workflow around your existing systems so the AI actually does the work — rather than sitting unused in a browser tab.

Tools are commodities. Workflows are the moat.

How long until I see results?

Phase 0 produces a working result on your real business data within a 30-day window, and the first AI agent is operational by the end of the pilot.

If it isn't, the Phase 0 fee is fully refunded — no questions asked.

Will this replace my staff?

Honest answer: no. AAA augments rather than replaces. It removes the admin and repetitive work that stops your staff from doing the work that actually pays.

Your team gets their day back. The owner gets their week back. Human judgment still matters.

What if it doesn't work?

Phase 0 is fully refundable for 30 days, no questions asked. If you don't see clear, measurable value within the pilot window, every cent comes back.

Build phases are paid only on milestone delivery — not upfront. If a phase isn't delivered, you don't pay for it. The retainer has a 3-month break clause. The risk sits with the operator at every step.
